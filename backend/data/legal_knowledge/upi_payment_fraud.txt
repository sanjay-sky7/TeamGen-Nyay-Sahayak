CRIME CATEGORY: UPI PAYMENT FRAUD

1. CRIME DESCRIPTION:
This incident occurs when a scammer tricks a person into sending money using UPI apps such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or BHIM. The fraud often involves fake payment screenshots, misleading “collect request” notifications, or impersonation.

2. IMMEDIATE STEPS (ACTION ROADMAP):
- Contact your bank and request transaction hold.
- Report the fraud via https://cybercrime.gov.in.
- Call Cyber Helpline 1930 to register the case.
- Change UPI PIN and secure bank passwords.

3. APPLICABLE BNS / IPC / OTHER LEGAL SECTIONS:
- BNS 318 (formerly IPC 420) Cheating.
- BNS 314 (formerly IPC 417) Simple Cheating.
- IT Act 66D: Impersonation using electronic means.

4. FIR PROCEDURE:
- Submit online complaint at https://cybercrime.gov.in.
- Then visit Police Station/Cyber Cell to convert to FIR.

5. EVIDENCE TO COLLECT:
- Transaction ID, chat history, screenshots, bank statement.

6. AUTHORITIES TO CONTACT:
- Cyber Helpline: 1930
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

7. MAIL DRAFT:
Subject: Complaint About UPI Fraud Transaction
I, <Name>, was defrauded of Rs. <Amount> by UPI transaction on <Date>. Evidence attached. Please take action.
