CRIME CATEGORY: FAKE JOB PORTAL / WORK-FROM-HOME SCAM

1. CRIME DESCRIPTION:
Fraudulent job offers requesting registration fees.

2. STEPS:
- Stop contact and report to https://cybercrime.gov.in.
- Inform bank to investigate recovery.

3. SECTIONS:
- BNS 318 (formerly IPC 420) Cheating.
- IT Act 66D.

4. FIR:
- File complaint online and then local Cyber Cell.

5. EVIDENCE:
- Payment receipt, ads, chats.

6. CONTACT:
- Cyber Helpline: 1930
