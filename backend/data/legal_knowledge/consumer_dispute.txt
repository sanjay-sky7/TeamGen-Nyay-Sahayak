CRIME CATEGORY: CONSUMER DISPUTE

1. DESCRIPTION:
Defective goods or deficient service.

2. STEPS:
- Contact seller support.
- Escalate to grievance officer.
- File complaint: https://consumerhelpline.gov.in.

3. SECTIONS:
- Consumer Protection Act 2019.

4. PROCEDURE:
- File case via https://edaakhil.nic.in.

5. EVIDENCE:
- Invoice, chats, photos.

6. CONTACT:
- NCH: 1800-11-4000 / 1915
