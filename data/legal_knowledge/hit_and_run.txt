CRIME CATEGORY: HIT AND RUN

1. DESCRIPTION:
Driver flees after causing accident.

2. STEPS:
- Call 112; record vehicle details.

3. SECTIONS:
- BNS and Motor Vehicles Act (negligent driving provisions).

4. FIR:
- File at local police or Zero FIR.

5. EVIDENCE:
- Photos, CCTV, witness contacts.

6. CONTACT:
- 112 / 108 / Traffic Police
