CRIME CATEGORY: ONLINE BLACKMAIL / EXTORTION

1. DESCRIPTION:
Threats demanding money to avoid release of personal content.

2. STEPS:
- Do not pay.
- Save chat/call logs.
- Report at https://cybercrime.gov.in.

3. SECTIONS:
- BNS 349 (Extortion)
- BNS 351 (Criminal Intimidation)
- IT Act 67A/67B (if sexual content)

4. FIR:
- Zero FIR allowed at any police station.

5. EVIDENCE:
- Screenshots, calls, account links.

6. CONTACT:
- 1930 / 112
