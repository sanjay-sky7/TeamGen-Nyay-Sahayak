CRIME CATEGORY: OTP THEFT / FAKE VERIFICATION CALL

1. CRIME DESCRIPTION:
Scammers pretend to be bank/company officials and ask for OTP to gain account access.

2. IMMEDIATE STEPS:
- Do not share OTP.
- Change banking/email passwords.
- Call your bank to freeze your account temporarily.

3. LEGAL SECTIONS:
- IT Act 66C (Identity Theft)
- IT Act 66D (Electronic Impersonation)
- BNS 316 (formerly IPC 419) Impersonation.

4. FIR PROCEDURE:
- File complaint on https://cybercrime.gov.in.
- Visit Police Station to convert to FIR.

5. EVIDENCE TO COLLECT:
- Call logs, screenshots, SMS alerts.

6. CONTACT:
- Cyber Helpline: 1930
- Police: 112
