CRIME CATEGORY: SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNT HACKING

1. DESCRIPTION:
Unauthorized access to social media accounts.

2. IMMEDIATE STEPS:
- Reset password and enable two-factor authentication.
- Report account as hacked in platform support.

3. SECTIONS:
- IT Act 66C, 66D
- BNS 336 (Defamation if misuse)

4. FIR:
- File complaint at https://cybercrime.gov.in.

5. EVIDENCE:
- Login logs and screenshots.

6. CONTACT:
- Cyber Helpline: 1930
